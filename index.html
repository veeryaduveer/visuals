[ 03 · Rules ]

The Code of War

Read carefully. Violation of any rule results in immediate disqualification — no exceptions.

01

Eligibility

All players must be 13 years or older. Squads must be registered with valid in-game IDs. No mid-tournament substitutions allowed once the bracket is locked.

02

Squad Composition

Each squad must consist of exactly four (4) players. A team captain is required and serves as the single point of contact for all official communication.

03

Fair Play Policy

Use of any third-party software, hacks, scripts, aim-bots, wall-hacks, sensitivity boosters, or modded clients results in permanent ban from this and all future Veer Gaming events.

04

Device Restrictions

Mobile devices only. PC emulators (BlueStacks, GameLoop, etc.) are strictly prohibited. iPad and tablet usage requires prior written approval from organisers.

05

Punctuality

Squads must report to the lobby at least 10 minutes before scheduled match start. Squads absent at the 5-minute mark forfeit their slot for that round.

06

Scoring System

Booyah: +15 points. Each kill: +1 point. Placement points awarded based on final standing per round. Combined points across all rounds determine the champion squad.

07

Toxicity & Conduct

Hate speech, slurs, harassment, intentional team-killing or griefing of any kind will not be tolerated. Decisions made by the moderation team are final.

08

Disconnection Rule

Network or device issues during a match are the player's own responsibility. Matches will not be paused or restarted due to individual disconnections.

09

Streaming & Content

Players may stream their own POV with a minimum 2-minute delay. Spectator-mode streaming is restricted to officially appointed Veer Gaming casters only.

10

Final Authority

Veer Gaming reserves the right to amend, modify, or interpret any rule at any time. All disputes are resolved by the organiser, and decisions are final and binding.
